# NISHTA IAS Academy — Design System

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Editorial authority meets a high-performance academic dashboard. Warm parchment gives the brand humility and humanity; deep navy communicates institutional trust; muted gold is the only accent.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

| Token | Value | Role |
|---|---:|---|
| primary | #CAA75D | CTAs, focus, links, progress only |
| canvas | #F4F0E7 | Main page background |
| surface-1 | #FBFAF6 | Elevated editorial surfaces |
| surface-2 | #EBE5DA | Secondary cards |
| ink | #071523 | Primary text and dark sections |
| ink-muted | #65717B | Supporting copy |

## 3. Typography Rules

Cormorant Garamond is the display face with tight tracking from -2px to -5px. Manrope is used for all body, navigation, labels, and metrics. Display headlines are concise and high-contrast; body copy is never tightly tracked.

## 4. Component Stylings

Buttons, panels, and cards use a consistent 3px precision radius. CTAs are solid gold or navy. Cards rely on hairline borders and surface changes; selected hero panels may use one offset architectural shadow.

## 5. Layout Principles

Mobile-first, asymmetric editorial grids, generous whitespace, left-aligned reading content, and a maximum 1180px content width. Touch targets are at least 44px.

## 6. Depth & Elevation

Depth uses perspective transforms, layered borders, and offset architectural planes. UI cards may tilt gently on desktop; the hero itself contains no decorative 3D scene.

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

- Use muted gold as the only accent.
- Use one 3px radius grammar.
- Keep all large display tracking negative.
- Keep body copy left-aligned and readable.
- Do not add gradient buttons, multi-accent decoration, generic stock imagery, or floating emoji icons.
- Do not place 3D decoration in the hero.

## 8. Responsive Behavior

Two-column editorial layouts stack below 960px. Navigation becomes a keyboard-accessible menu. Cards lose perspective transforms under reduced-motion and at narrow viewports.

## 9. Agent Prompt Guide

Design NISHTA as a restrained, premium UPSC institution: parchment canvas, near-black navy, one muted-gold accent, serif display typography, analytical UI details, and subtle architectural 3D depth outside the hero.
