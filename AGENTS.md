# NISHTA IAS Website Guardrails

- Use `#CAA75D` as the only accent; reserve it for calls to action, focus, links, and progress.
- Use 3px radii consistently. Do not introduce pills.
- Maintain negative display tracking and Manrope body copy.
- Use surface ladders, hairlines, perspective, and at most one offset shadow per composition.
- Do not introduce 3D decorative objects in the hero.
- Respect reduced-motion preferences and 44px touch targets.
- Keep all business details and contact actions accurate.
- Do not add a backend; forms and enquiries route to telephone or external services.
